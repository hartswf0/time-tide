Project Evolution

This project explores how we can transform our relationship with creative technology. Hyperclay reimagines computation as a flowing material we can shape with our intentions, rather than a rigid set of interfaces and tools. We envision a creative environment where boundaries dissolve, possibilities multiply, and your creative intent directly shapes the digital medium. Our goal is to build pathways toward a future where creators have true sovereignty over the computational systems they use.

Applications & Tools

Slit Scan Visualization

Our web-based time-manipulation tool reveals temporal dimensions hidden within spatial movement. By folding time into space, it visualizes reality as a fluid continuum rather than discrete moments, challenging traditional representations of movement and transforming video into a medium for ontological exploration.
